recent projects

Studypods

study pods is an initiative taken by dsc mbm to promote peer-to-peer learning among the students with a notion that says “learn together, build together”.the program is designed to offer participants a good exposure of technology and the technical world. it is an approx two month-long program that connects like-minded participants interested in a particular domain to come together, learn together and then build together. pod mentors assist participants with the resources and take weekly updates. at the end of the program each study pod submits a project or a report that showcases their learnings during the program.

DSC Website

in this project, we made a robust website for the developer student community of mbm, jnvu. it is a one-stop solution, where you can find rich resources for all the major technologies. it not only has a detailed roadmap for aspiring tech-savvies but also is an amazing place to view and showcase projects. you can get the latest updates on all the upcoming events and can even host your own events!
